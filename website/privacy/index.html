Privacy Policy

Effective date: [EFFECTIVE_DATE]  ·  Questions: [DPO_EMAIL]

1. Who We Are

Hookwing provides webhook infrastructure for developers and AI agents. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it. We keep this plain because we would want the same if we were reading it.

Data Controller: [COMPANY_LEGAL_NAME], [COMPANY_ADDRESS]. Contact: [DPO_EMAIL].

Registration Number: [COMPANY_NUMBER]

2. What Data We Collect

Account Data: When you create an account, we collect your email address. If you provide it, we also collect your name. For paid plans, we collect billing information including your company name, billing address, and payment details (processed securely by Stripe).

Usage Data: We collect data about your use of the Service, including API request volumes, endpoint configurations, event delivery logs, error rates, feature adoption, and interaction patterns. This helps us operate and improve the Service.

Event Payload Data: The webhook payloads you send through Hookwing. We process this data solely to deliver it to your configured destinations. We do not read, analyze, mine, or use the content of webhook payloads for any purpose other than delivery. Payload content is never used for advertising or sold to third parties.

Technical Data: Automatically collected data including IP addresses, user agent strings, HTTP headers, timestamps, and geographic location data (derived from IP addresses). This is collected for security monitoring, abuse prevention, and troubleshooting.

Communication Data: If you contact us via email or through our support systems, we retain those communications to respond to your inquiries and improve our Service.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Article 6)

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

(a) Performance of a Contract: We process account data, usage data, and event payload data as necessary to provide the webhook infrastructure Service you request under our Terms of Service.

(b) Legitimate Interests: We process technical data for security monitoring, fraud prevention, and maintaining the integrity of our Service. This processing is necessary for our legitimate interests in protecting the Service and our users.

(c) Legal Obligation: We process certain data to comply with legal obligations, including tax and accounting requirements.

(d) Consent: For marketing communications (which we do not currently send), we would rely on your consent. We do not send marketing emails at this time.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Receiving, processing, and delivering webhook payloads to your configured endpoints; managing delivery retries; maintaining delivery logs; providing dashboard access
  • Account Management: Communicating about your account, billing, security notifications, and service changes
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: Detecting and preventing abuse, fraud, unauthorized access, and security incidents
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory, tax, and accounting obligations
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to identify improvements, but never sharing this analysis externally

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your event payload data with third parties except as necessary for delivery.

5. Data Retention Periods

Event Payload Data: Retained according to your subscription plan's retention window. You can configure retention settings or delete events at any time via the dashboard or API. After account termination, event data is deleted within 30 days.

Account Data: Retained for the duration of your active subscription and for a period of three (3) years afterward to satisfy legal and tax obligations, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

Billing Records: Retained for seven (7) years as required by tax and accounting regulations.

Technical Data: Retained for ninety (90) days for security and debugging purposes.

Communication Records: Retained for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiries and for two (2) years thereafter for quality assurance purposes.

You can request earlier deletion of your data at any time by contacting [DPO_EMAIL].

6. Third-Party Data Processors

We use the following third-party services to operate Hookwing. Each provider is bound by data processing agreements:

  • Cloudflare, Inc. — Hosting, content delivery network (CDN), DDoS protection, and edge computing. Data is processed on Cloudflare's global network. Privacy Policy
  • Stripe, Inc. — Payment processing for paid plans. We do not store credit card numbers on our systems; all payment data is handled directly by Stripe. Privacy Policy
  • Resend, Inc. — Transactional email delivery for account communications. Privacy Policy

These providers are located in the United States and are subject to standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission for international data transfers.

We do not share personal data with any other third parties. We do not use data brokers, advertising networks, or analytics providers that resell data.

7. International Data Transfers

The Service is delivered through Cloudflare's global edge network. Your data may be processed in any country where Cloudflare operates, including the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, European Union member states, Australia, and Japan.

All data transfers are protected by:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+)
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) with all third-party processors
  • Cloudflare's compliance with EU-US Data Privacy Framework and UK Extension

For users in the European Economic Area, your data is processed in compliance with GDPR Chapter V (Transfers).

8. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.

We use a single session cookie to maintain your authenticated state when logged into the dashboard. This is a strictly necessary cookie required for the Service to function. It is deleted when you close your browser.

We do not use:

  • Third-party analytics scripts (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Advertising cookies or pixels
  • Tracking cookies
  • Persistent cookies beyond the session

No cookie consent banner is required as no non-essential cookies are used.

9. Your Data Rights

Under GDPR (for EU/UK users), CCPA (for California users), and other applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights:

Right of Access (Article 15 GDPR): You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification (Article 16 GDPR): You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.

Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten", Article 17 GDPR): You may request deletion of your personal data. Note: we may retain certain data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes as described in Section 5.

Right to Data Portability (Article 20 GDPR): You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Object (Article 21 GDPR): You may object to processing based on legitimate interests.

Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to file a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email [DPO_EMAIL]. We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR. For California residents, we respond within 45 days as required by CCPA.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from, children under the age of 16. If you become aware that a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at [DPO_EMAIL] and we will delete such data.

11. Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data is transmitted over TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive data is encrypted using AES-256
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control limits data access to authorized personnel
  • Signing Secrets: Webhook signing secrets are never logged or exposed in API responses
  • Network Security: DDoS protection via Cloudflare, Web Application Firewall (WAF)
  • Employee Training: Staff undergo annual security and privacy training

To report a security vulnerability, contact security@hookwing.com. We operate a responsible disclosure program.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the Service evolves. Material changes (changes that significantly affect your rights) will be communicated by email at least 14 days before taking effect.

Minor changes (such as updating contact details or clarifying existing practices) may be made without notice.

The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
